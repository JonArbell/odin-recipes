PORK STEAK



Pork steak is a hearty and satisfying dish made with tender pork steaks seasoned with salt, pepper, soy sauce, and Worcestershire sauce. The addition of minced garlic infuses the meat with aromatic flavor, while vegetable oil ensures a crispy exterior when pan-fried. Simmering the pork steaks in water or beef broth creates a flavorful sauce, perfect for spooning over the tender meat. Optional garnishes like sliced onions and mushrooms add an extra layer of taste and visual appeal to this classic dish.


Here's a recipe for pork steak along with its ingredients and descriptions:

  • Pork Steaks:

    Pork steaks, typically cut from the shoulder or loin, are the main ingredient in this dish. They offer a juicy and flavorful protein base for the meal.

  • Salt and Pepper:

    These basic seasonings are used to enhance the flavor of the pork steaks, providing a simple yet delicious taste.

  • Soy Sauce:

    Soy sauce adds a savory umami flavor to the pork steaks and helps to tenderize the meat during cooking.

  • Worcestershire Sauce:

    Worcestershire sauce contributes to the depth of flavor in the dish, providing a slightly tangy and complex taste to the pork steaks.

  • Garlic:

    Minced garlic adds aromatic richness to the marinade, infusing the pork steaks with a delicious garlicky flavor.

  • Vegetable Oil:

    Vegetable oil is used for frying the pork steaks, helping to achieve a crispy exterior while keeping the meat juicy on the inside.

  • Water or Beef Broth:

    Water or beef broth is added to the skillet to create a flavorful sauce and prevent the pork steaks from drying out during cooking.

  • Optional Garnishes:

    Sliced onions and mushrooms can be added to the dish for extra flavor and texture, enhancing the overall presentation of the pork steaks.


Here's a step-by-step procedure to make pork steak:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:

    • Gather the ingredients: pork steaks, salt, pepper, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, minced garlic, vegetable oil, water or beef broth, and optional garnishes like sliced onions and mushrooms.

  2. Season the Pork Steaks:

    • Season both sides of the pork steaks with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences.

  3. Marinate the Pork:

    • In a bowl or resealable plastic bag, combine soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and minced garlic.

    • Add the pork steaks to the marinade, ensuring they are evenly coated. Marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

  4. Cook the Pork Steaks:

    • Heat vegetable oil in a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat.
    • Once the oil is hot, add the marinated pork steaks to the skillet.
    • Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are browned and cooked through.

  5. Add Liquid and Simmer:

    • Pour water or beef broth into the skillet to deglaze and create a flavorful sauce.
    • Allow the liquid to come to a simmer, and let the pork steaks cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to thicken the sauce slightly.

  6. Optional Garnish and Serve:

    • If desired, add sliced onions and mushrooms to the skillet during the last few minutes of cooking for extra flavor and texture.
    • Once the pork steaks are cooked through and the sauce has thickened, remove them from the skillet and transfer to a serving platter.
    • Spoon any remaining sauce over the pork steaks and serve hot with your favorite side dishes.

Enjoy your delicious homemade Pork Steak! Adjust the seasonings and ingredients according to your taste preferences for a personalized touch.
